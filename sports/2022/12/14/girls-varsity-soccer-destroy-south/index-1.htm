Girl’s Varsity Soccer Destroy South

Saharah Hall

Zoe Baker (11) dribbling the ball down field

Saharah Hall, Reporter

The girl’s varsity team destroyed South 10-0 on 4/26 with amazing performances from Zoe Baker (11), Payton Benoit (11), Lola Reida (9), and Angie D. Rodriguez-Guzman (10). The girls have currently won 7 and lost only 4 with an incoming senior night where we will go against the rivalry Bishop Carroll.

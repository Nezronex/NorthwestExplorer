Auditions – Northwest Explorer https://www.northwestexplorer.org The Student Voice of Wichita Northwest High School Thu, 13 Sep 2018 20:01:00 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=6.3.1 North and Northwest students audition for “Grease” https://www.northwestexplorer.org/news/2014/09/05/north-and-northwest-students-audition-for-grease/ https://www.northwestexplorer.org/news/2014/09/05/north-and-northwest-students-audition-for-grease/#respond Fri, 05 Sep 2014 20:38:19 +0000 http://www.northwestexplorer.org/?p=3051 Auditions for the North/Northwest collaboration of “Grease” were held in the Northwest auditorium on Aug. 21 and 22.

“I think it’s going to be a cool project,” North drama teacher Tyrone Galbreath said. “Lundin and I have been friends for a while so we thought we’d try this out and I think it’ll be kind of cool to bring two schools together and work on a project.”

Over 100 kids auditioned in the two day period.


“Auditions are taking a little longer than I wanted but that’s really a good thing,” Northwest drama teacher Richard Lundin said. “That means that lots of kids are showing up.”

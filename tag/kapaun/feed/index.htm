Kapaun scored a touchdown within the first 8 minutes of the game. Northwest committed four turnovers that led Kapaun to score 20 points. “To win early season games you can’t make mistakes.  We handed them the win,” head coach Steve Martin said. “We will continue to do what we do and eliminate mistakes.”

The Crusaders defeated Northwest, 6-26.

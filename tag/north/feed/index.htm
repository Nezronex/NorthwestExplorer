“I think it’s going to be a cool project,” North drama teacher Tyrone Galbreath said. “Lundin and I have been friends for a while so we thought we’d try this out and I think it’ll be kind of cool to bring two schools together and work on a project.”

Over 100 kids auditioned in the two day period.


“Auditions are taking a little longer than I wanted but that’s really a good thing,” Northwest drama teacher Richard Lundin said. “That means that lots of kids are showing up.”

]]> https://www.northwestexplorer.org/news/2014/09/05/north-and-northwest-students-audition-for-grease/feed/ 0 Northwest Girls Basketball 2-0 to Start the Season https://www.northwestexplorer.org/sports/2013/12/17/northwest-girls-basketball-2-0-to-start-the-season/ https://www.northwestexplorer.org/sports/2013/12/17/northwest-girls-basketball-2-0-to-start-the-season/#respond Tue, 17 Dec 2013 18:24:51 +0000 http://www.northwestexplorer.org/?p=2581 The season is back for Northwest basketball and our girls are starting out with a fire to win.

The Northwest girls won their first game 53-33 against North on Dec. 6, and demolished West in their second game  with a 67-21 score on Dec. 10.

“It’s always a great experience,” sophomore Shaunece Grant said on her first year playing with the varsity team.

The girls have started off great so far and are improving in their practices.

“So far as a team we have gotten better each game, so I hope that continues,” Grant said.

“We’ve been turning the ball over a lot so we have been focusing on keeping the ball in practice, I think our hard work will pay off in our next game against South,” sophomore Haley Cooper said.

Their next game is on Tuesday, Dec. 17 at home against South.

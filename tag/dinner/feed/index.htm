The event will be located in the Northwest commons, from 6 p.m. to 8 p.m. There will be a fee of $10 per person, and $5 for children under the age of 5.

Proceeds will go to Concert Choir to attend the Heritage Festival in Chicago, IL.

During the dinner there will be entertainment by Concert Choir.

You can pre-order your meal at the door or by calling (316)973-6043. To buy a ticket contact a Concert Choir member or purchase one at the door when you arrive. They will only accept cash/check.

Menu includes:

fettuccine alfredo

bread sticks/salad

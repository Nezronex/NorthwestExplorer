GfC – Northwest Explorer https://www.northwestexplorer.org The Student Voice of Wichita Northwest High School Thu, 13 Sep 2018 20:01:41 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=6.3.1 Pay it Forward campaign https://www.northwestexplorer.org/news/2014/01/31/pay-it-forward-campaign/ https://www.northwestexplorer.org/news/2014/01/31/pay-it-forward-campaign/#respond Fri, 31 Jan 2014 18:03:04 +0000 http://www.northwestexplorer.org/?p=2652 The Grizzlies for Change Pay it Forward Campaign started Thursday, Jan. 23. The campaign involves doing nice things for people and handing out bracelets that tell them to “Pay it Forward.” Then the people who performed the good deed go to the library and write what they did on a piece of paper.

“I’d like to cover all the windows in the library,” said Grizzlies for Change sponsor Brian Latta.

The campaign has no set end date.

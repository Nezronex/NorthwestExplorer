Scholars Bowl – Northwest Explorer https://www.northwestexplorer.org The Student Voice of Wichita Northwest High School Thu, 13 Sep 2018 20:01:19 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=6.3.1 Scholars Bowl goes to state https://www.northwestexplorer.org/features/2014/03/10/scholars-bowl-goes-to-state/ https://www.northwestexplorer.org/features/2014/03/10/scholars-bowl-goes-to-state/#respond Mon, 10 Mar 2014 17:14:21 +0000 http://www.northwestexplorer.org/?p=2755 On Feb. 15 sophomores Chris Kliewer, Marie Altgilbers, and Emily Dulohery and seniors Josue Quintana, Taryn Powers, and Kylie Norton competed in the state Scholars Bowl tournament. Out of 8 teams the Northwest team came in last. However, this is the first time in at least 11 years that Northwest has qualified for state. It doesn’t take much to be a part of Scholars Bowl.

“Scholars Bowl is a team of academically talented students, and we go to tournaments, and we answer questions about lots of topics,” said Dulohery.

Anyone is welcome to join Scholars Bowl; there are no tryouts.

It takes a lot of practice to make it to state. The questions are harder, and it’s a difficult challenge for the Northwest team. The Northwest team practiced once a week, and ate lots of pizza. Although the team was disappointed in the results, they were happy they made it, and enjoyed the day.

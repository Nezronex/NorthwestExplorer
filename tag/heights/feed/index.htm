Heights – Northwest Explorer https://www.northwestexplorer.org The Student Voice of Wichita Northwest High School Thu, 13 Sep 2018 20:01:41 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=6.3.1 Northwest Wrestlers win again https://www.northwestexplorer.org/sports/2014/01/31/northwest-wrestlers-win-again/ https://www.northwestexplorer.org/sports/2014/01/31/northwest-wrestlers-win-again/#respond Fri, 31 Jan 2014 20:58:21 +0000 http://www.northwestexplorer.org/?p=2664 Northwest’s wrestling team has been accomplishing all they set out for so far this season. The team is 5-0 in the City League. “We have eight kids ranked for state already, so I’d say that we’re doing well,” wrestling coach Eric Prichard said. The team has won every tournament they have been to so far including Wellington, Pratt, and Bishop Carrol. “The team is right where I want them to be right now,” Prichard said. Northwest is going for their 3rd straight City League championship. “I really want us to go for a three-peat, I don’t think its been done before yet,” Prichard said. The wrestlers most recently had a 49-18 win against Heights on Wednesday, Jan. 29. “I’m looking forward to finishing the year strong, and really hoping for a top three finish at state this year,” Prichard said.

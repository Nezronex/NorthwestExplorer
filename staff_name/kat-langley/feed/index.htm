It’s finally over. Breaking Dawn Part Two, after seeing all the movies and reading all the books I think that Breaking dawn Part Two was a great way to end the series.
It had a lot  of action and also a happy ending, although my only two problems with the movie was the CGI, it felt like I was watching graphics from 2001, I mean I know that Twilight makes tons of money why not have spent it on some better CGI?
The other was the bad acting it was very over dramatic but only towards the beginning after they brought it more characters it got better, other than that it was a good movie.
Now if you’re not a fan of Twilight I wouldn’t recommend seeing it. But if you are, you will love it.
Another nice touch at the end of the movie had credits from everyone in all of the movies from the Twilight saga.
I would rate the movie a 3 out of 5.  But I would honestly would wait to see it on DVD instead of in the movies because it was a good movie but not worth seeing in the theater.

Well, there are lots of reasons why I like to dress up and why I think other people should dress up to school. Dressing up is a way to make myself comfortable. Most people would say heels and skirts etc., aren’t comfortable, but I’m not saying you have to wear those things.
You can still dress up and be comfortable. Later in life we’re going to have to grow up and go out into the world and dress for success, we’re going to have to wear suits, ties, heels, skirts, dresses why not start now, unless you work at McDonald’s and even then you still have to wear a nice pear of khakis.
Fashion is going to stay with us for the rest of our lives, it’s never going to go. There’s always going to be something new, something better and even more trendy then before. I believe that we as teenagers express ourselves in the way we dress. when you meet someone new 1st impration is everything. a 1st inpration only takes about 30 sec and to change that 1st inpretion takes about 5 min. The way you dress should be a reflection of how you feel and what kind of person you are.
